## Adaptive Narrative Publishing Platform

---

# 1. PRODUCT NAME

## byDebut

Tagline:
One Story. Infinite Audiences.

Secondary positioning:
Adaptive Narrative Publishing Platform

---

# 2. CORE MVP IDEA

byDebut allows users to:

* transform stories into alternate versions,
* adapt books for different audiences,
* create illustrated children editions,
* and discover which audiences emotionally connect most with each version.

The platform demonstrates how one book can evolve into multiple commercially viable editions.

IMPORTANT:
The MVP is NOT:

* an AI writing assistant,
* a publishing marketplace,
* or a social network.

The MVP IS:

# a cinematic demonstration of the future of adaptive publishing.

---

# 3. PRIMARY HACKATHON GOAL

The judges must understand THIS in less than 60 seconds:

## A single book can become:

* alternate narrative versions,
* children editions,
* family editions,
* educational editions,
* and audience-optimized adaptations.

That is the core breakthrough.

# OBJECTIVE OF STEP 2

Create the first complete transformation experience for a public domain story.

The experience must demonstrate:

1. Structural story analysis.
2. Identification of critical variables.
3. Narrative transformation.
4. Forking visualization.
5. Audience discovery.
6. Narrative optimization.
7. Final pitch generation.

Use Frankenstein as the demo story.

---

## What Is byDebut?

byDebut is a platform where stories evolve.

A place where readers can create new versions of a novel by changing one important variable from the original story.

This is not simply about writing something different.

It is about discovering how one small change can completely transform:

* characters,
* societies,
* governments,
* relationships,
* religions,
* wars,
* technologies,
* and even the future of humanity.

---

## Because Every Story Contains Hidden Stories

What would have happened if one decision had been different?

What if death disappeared?

What if one single idea changed an entire civilization?

# How byDebut Works

When you enter:

## [www.byDebut.com](http://www.byDebut.com)

you will follow a very simple process.

---

## STEP 1

### Find This Novel

Inside byDebut, you will find the book you have just finished reading.

You will see:

* the cover,
* the description,
* the central ideas of the story,
* and the main variables that shape the universe of the novel.

---

## STEP 2

### Choose What You Want to Change

Now comes the most important question:

> What would happen if something were different?

You will be able to modify an important variable from the story.

For example:

* a law,
* a technology,
* a relationship,
* a decision,
* a discovery,
* a war,
* an emotion,
* or even human nature itself.

---

## STEP 3

### Describe Your Idea

You do not need to be a writer.

You only need to explain your idea in a simple way.

For example:

* “What if immortality existed only for the wealthy?”
* “What if nobody were legally allowed to die?”
* “What if artificial intelligence replaced governments?”
* “What if love could last for centuries?”
* “What if human memory could be edited?”

---

## STEP 4

### byDebut Creates a New Version

The system will begin building a new reality based on your change.

It will generate:

* a new premise,
* a new narrative direction,
* new conflicts,
* new consequences,
* and eventually entirely new chapters.

The story will begin evolving.

---

## STEP 5

### Save and Share Your Version

You will be able to:

* save your version,
* continue developing it,
* share it,
* compare it with others,
* and explore versions created by other readers.

---

## STEP 6

### Explore Alternative Worlds

Each new version can generate even more versions.

One story can transform into hundreds of different possibilities.

Some will become darker.

Some more human.

Some more dangerous.

And some may become more real than we imagine.
